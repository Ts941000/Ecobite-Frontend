India's Food Waste Crisis

India wastes nearly 68 million tonnes of food every year — enough to feed the entire population of a country. Meanwhile, millions go hungry.

We founded EcoBite in 2024 with a simple but powerful idea: what if the surplus meals from India's finest hotels — meals that are perfectly fresh, delicious, and safe — could reach people who'd love to eat them, at prices that make everyone smile?

From a small pilot in Delhi, EcoBite has grown into a movement across 32 cities, partnering with over 840 premium hotels to rescue surplus meals before they go to waste.
