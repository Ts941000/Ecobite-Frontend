EcoBite Raises ₹45 Crore in Series B to Expand Food Rescue Network to 50 Cities

EcoBite today announced the closing of its Series B funding round led by Sequoia India and Climate Capital Partners. The funds will be used to expand operations from 32 to 50 cities by Q1 2027, strengthen the cold-chain logistics network, and enhance AI-driven demand prediction technology.

EcoBite Partners with Taj Hotels Group to Rescue 10,000 Meals Monthly

In a landmark partnership, EcoBite has signed a nationwide agreement with the Taj Hotels Group, enabling surplus meal rescue across 85 Taj properties. The partnership is expected to prevent over 120 tonnes of food waste annually and save customers an estimated ₹8 crore per year.

EcoBite Crosses 2 Lakh Meals Rescued Milestone, Prevents 400 Tonnes of CO₂

EcoBite today celebrated the rescue of its 200,000th meal, achieving the milestone in just 18 months of operations. The cumulative environmental impact includes preventing over 400 tonnes of CO₂ emissions — equivalent to taking 180 cars off the road for a year.

EcoBite was recognized as "Startup of the Year" at the prestigious India Sustainability Awards 2025 for its innovative approach to food waste reduction. The jury praised EcoBite's technology-driven model and measurable environmental impact across India's hospitality sector.
